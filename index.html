About

At Rey Industries we pride ourselves in our creativity and ability to innovate on our past designs. Whether it be altering a childs toy into a motorized vehicle propeled by a fan or a spice bottle being turned into a flashlight, we won't be stopped by a seemingly simple base. Our creations are only limited by our imagination, even simple templates or requests will be turned into complex patterns and designs in order to maximize creativity with the canvas. Even our drawings tend to go from just a few lines to a shaded and detailed design to show off our full abilities.

Rey Industries is ran by the student and creator of this website. They are more than willing to be commisioned for drawn works, painted works, programmed devices, custom built devices, and more! Whether it be programming a simple stop light or drawing a picture of almost anything your heart might conjure up, I can do it. If you're interested in my work please leave information to contact you below and I will be in touch as soon as I can given other/prior constraints.

Painted works

We at Rey Inudstries are quite familiar with painting objects. If you want a painting commissioned piece, please discuss it with our representative (or just enter a contact form below). We will respond within at least a business week's time.


In your contact form (or discussion with a representative), please have an idea of what you would like to have painted and the aspect ratio for the piece.

Drawn Works

If you want a creation/piece/work drawn up by Rey Industries, we will need as many details about the subject of the work as we can. If the piece is a person, scene, or object that you are not wanting blueprints drawn up, please inform us in the contact form or representative you speak with. Otherwise, the artists will need exact measurements of at least three significant figures of measurement (for example Length: 1.001 inches, Width: 1.001 inches, Height: 1.001 inches).


If your desired piece is of a person, please have at least two reference pieces and the desired details/modifications you want for the piece. If you want the piece in a specific color scheme or style, please let the artist or representative know before discussing the aspect ratio.

Modification Works

If you want something You own modified by any of our workers, please fill out a contact form or talk to a representative. Within the contact form, please enter your desired changes and where you would like the parts to come from. If the technician/worker you want cannot complete the alterations, the worker (or representative) you've requested will (more than likely) re-assign the modification to the most capable worker/technician we have. If we cannot fulfill your alteration desires, we will redirect you to another shop where we feel you will get the best results and minimal costs locally available.

Game projects

If you want a game or game asset created or modified by our artists here at Rey Industries, please disclose your hopes for the game's story, parts, art style, characters, and any destructibility within the environment.


If you want several game assets created or modified to fit a style or design, please talk with the artists about the stylization and color schemes you are hope to achieve.


Pricing will depend on changes, time, and effort of the artist/(s) to reach customer satisfaction. The artist/(s) that thakes up the commission will be in moderated contact with the customer for mutual comfort and safety.

Videography/Animated Projects

If you want to commission any of our creatives for videography or animated projects, please fill out a contact form explaining the full scope of the project, time, and casting choices you would like to hire. If casting choices are unwilling/unavailable for the project, we will do our best to ease the inconvenience of another creative willing to join your project or financial compensation for scheduling issues.

Custom Creations

Should you want to commission our mechanics/technicians for a custom creation or something in their abilities to build for you (Strictly large/complex projects), please discuss this with a company representative or put the project's scope in the contact form. The information our talents require will depend on the scope of the tasks they will complete for this project, and pricing for these creations may enlist unexpected departments. If project requirements pass the original scope, we will attempt to contact you within at least three days of discovery. If the project requires departments outside the original scope, pricing will be negotiated with the customer. Should representatives be unsuccessful in their attempts, the project will be on hold, and the team you requested will attempt to reach you for no less than a week before halting entirely (if they cannot progress without the other department). Communication with the customer will become a must before the team can progress.
